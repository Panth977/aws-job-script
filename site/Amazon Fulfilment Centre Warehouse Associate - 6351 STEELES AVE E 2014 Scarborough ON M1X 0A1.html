    Amazon is hiring for the following types of roles in your area:

    Delivery Station – You’ll be part of the dedicated Amazon team at the delivery station – the last stop before we deliver smiles to customers. Delivery Station Associates receive trucks full of orders, then prepare them for delivery. You’ll load conveyor belts, and transport and stage deliveries to be picked up by drivers. To ensure we meet promised delivery times, shift times will vary. Depending on your location, you’ll work a fixed schedule with hours that range between full-time and part-time.

    XL WAREHOUSE – You’ll be part of the Amazon warehouse team that handles the big stuff – those unique customer orders that weigh over 50 pounds (22 kg): big screen TVs, furniture, appliances, and more. You’ll be trained on how to use technology to handle these heavy bulk items. Our teams work overnight shifts to meet promised delivery times during the day. You’ll work up to 19 hours per week, have a reliable pay rate, and no surprises.

    Fulfillment Centre – You’ll be part of the Amazon warehouse team that gets orders ready for customers relying on Amazon services. In this fast-paced role, you will be selecting, packing, and shipping orders that take place in a variety of areas, including merch, make-on-demand, customer returns, and general fulfillment. Work a full-time, fixed schedule. Shift options include overnight and days, and usually at least one weekend day.
